Report DNC Violation

This website uses your Gmail account to send a complaint to the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC). We will not store your personal information in any way. You can inspect the source code of this website on Github.

The form here is loosely based on the Microsoft Word-based form on PDPC's website, but aims to reduce the hassle of editing a MS Word document, attaching it to your email and then sending it to PDPC. Instead, you can do it all on this page with your smart phone!

We are not in any way affliated to the PDPC. You will need a fairly recent version of Chrome / Firefox / Safari. Report problems to xkjyeah@gmail.com :)
